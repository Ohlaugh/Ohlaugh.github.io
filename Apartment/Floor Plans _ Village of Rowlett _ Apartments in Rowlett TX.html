Pet Policy

Previous

  • 1

    Policy

    No Weight Limit.

    Limit 2 indoor pets per apartment. No exotic animals.

  • 2

    Fees

    Non-refundable pet fee of $350. for the first animal plus $350 for each additional animal.

  • 3

    Pet Rent

    Monthly Rent of $15/pet/month.

  • 4

    Breed Restrictions

    Excluded dog breeds include Akita, Alaskan Malamute, American Bull Dog, American Pit Bull Terrier, American or Bull Staffordshire Terrier, Bull Terrier, Chinese Shar-Pei, Dalmatian, Doberman Pinscher, Presa Canario, Pit Bull, Rottweiler, Siberian Husky, Stafford Terrier, Chow, German Shepherd and any mix thereof. Letter required by Certified Veterinarian for proof of breed, weight, and required vaccinations.

Village of Rowlett - Apartments in Rowlett, TX

Live in the historic area of Downtown Rowlett in the comfort of our apartments on Main Street in Rowlett, TX. Village of Rowlett combines a desirable location and high-end amenities for the best in luxury living.

Choose between a variety of unique studio, one, and two bedroom floor plans that are sure to fit any lifestyle. Each unit features designer interior finishes, like granite countertops and upscale lighting fixtures, that make modern living possible at our apartments in Rowlett, TX. Enjoy wood-style flooring throughout living spaces, tile kitchen backsplashes, tub surrounds, and private yards or patios for your convenience. Our Rowlett Apartments have Wi-Fi enabled common areas including a business center, pool courtyard with a sundeck, and a state-of-the-art resident lounge. Outdoors, our pet-friendly apartments have an open dog run, an outdoor living area with a fireplace and grills, and a community garden on site.

Our luxury apartments in Rowlett, TX are within walking distance from the DART Rail, which allows residents to easily travel into the heart of Dallas and the surrounding community. Herfurth Park, Rowlett Public Library, City Hall, and plenty of local restaurants and shops are just steps away from our Rowlett Apartments on Main Street. If youre looking for the best luxury apartments near Garland, TX, look no further than Village of Rowlett and schedule a tour today!
